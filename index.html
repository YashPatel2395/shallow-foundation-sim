Shallow Foundation

Learn the complete sequence of constructing a shallow foundation, including soil assessment, excavation, reinforcement, concrete placement, backfilling, and final column construction.

Learning Objectives
  • Understand shallow foundation construction sequence
  • Learn why most of the foundation system is below ground
  • Practice inspection and quality checks
  • Visualize footing, rebar, backfill, and pillar construction

Driven Pile Foundation

Learn how prefabricated piles are positioned and driven into the ground using a pile driving hammer until the required depth or resistance is reached.

Learning Objectives
  • Understand pile positioning and alignment
  • Learn pile driving using hammer impacts
  • Monitor depth and blow count
  • Understand pile cap construction
🪜 10 Steps 🕐 ~30 min

Drilled Shaft Foundation

Learn how a deep borehole is drilled, inspected, reinforced with a steel cage, and filled with concrete to create a drilled shaft foundation.

Learning Objectives
  • Understand borehole drilling and spoil removal
  • Learn reinforcement cage placement
  • Simulate concrete placement in a drilled shaft
  • Understand final shaft inspection
